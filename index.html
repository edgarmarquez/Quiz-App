What country was the first to win the World Cup?

Right Answer!!

Uruguay was the first crowned World Champion back in 1930. Uruguay beat Argentina 4-2 in the final. Uruguay won the championship one more time beating Brasil 2 -1 against all odds in 1950. That heroic performance was called "Maracanazo". The game was played at Maracana Stadium.
